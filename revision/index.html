We are change makers

Based out of the Interactive Media program at the University of Miami School of Communication, we are an award winning academic game studio that focuses on social impact. NERDLab integrates classic and novel techniques to produce projects that inspire and educate through innovation and fun.

Incubation

Good ideas can come from anyone. Projects developed by NERDLab aren't just dreamed up in our studio. They come from prototypes built in the classroom and workshops with research teams.

Innovation

As games continue to grow in the ways that they are used, we seek to push the boundaries of what can be done through this interactive medium: promoting critical dialogue, reducing stigmas, advocating for social justice, dissecting complex systems, and much more.

Professional Development

Students from across the University of Miami are invited to apply to be a part of NERDLab. We welcome anyone looking to level up their skills and increase their body of work. We think the best way to learn is by working on real world projects.

Mentorship

We are committed to providing opportunities for hands on learning with a focus on mentorship. We are a community and we strive to create life long relationships with the members of our lab.

Squawker

Research Prototype

Working with communication researchers, we developed a novel intervention to that aims to encourage? first year college students to identify signs of mental health issues.

Unsavory

Game

According to a 2012 survey of restaurant workers, 90% did not receive paid sick days. This Serious Play Gold Award winning game allows the player to experience the perspective of a person working in a fast casual restaurant when they get sick.

Zoo Rush

Game

Sickle Cell Disease is a painful condition that disproportionally affects African Americans. This AEJMC Best of Web? mobile game seeks to increase awareness and raise empathy due to the stigmas associated with chronic pain and drug abuse?

Atlantis

Game

What kind of game could be played by everyone in a movie theater? This 2014? IndieCade selection is composed of 4 mini games that tell the final story of the lost civilization of Atlantis.

Dreamy

Research Prototype

Can we use a game to change a college student's intention to use a tanning bed? Dreamy is a dating simulator that incorporates tanning into the attractiveness of characters. This creates the conditions to incorporate messages based on a behavioral theory model?

Don Grey Cafe

Game

First prototyped in a 24 hour game jam, this Horizon Interactive award winner was eventually released on the App Store and Google Play. We partnered with a local Miami woman owned business to incorporate their product into the game.

Dwell

Game

Developed in collaboration with nursing faculty, this poverty simulator is designed to allow nursing students to experience the health care system through the perspective of a patient. This game will soon be published through Central Michigan Press?

Eradicate!

Game

First, there was Humans vs. Mosquitoes, a physical game comissioned by Red Cross Red Crescent to teach children how to prevent mosquitoes from breeding. This iteration of the game is designed as a card game to allow for play in small groups or as a solo activity.

Cops and Rubbers

Game

Did you know that there are countries around the world where the police use the possession of a condom as evidence of prostitution? In this Meaningful Play award winning game, you play as a sex worker with the constant threat of the police arresting you for protecting yourself.

Vanity

Game

Before Dreamy, there was Vanity: a board game that puts aspiring Hollywood actors in auditions that demand a specific shade of skin. Get the part, but don't get skin cancer!

Personal Exposure Reporter

Web App

One of 7? initial projects from the Firefighter Cancer Initiative, this app allows firefighters to log their exposure to toxic elements and share the data with researchers. By collecting enough data, the team hopes that evidence is found to advocate for legislation that acknowledges the increased risk of specific cancers for firefighters.

SCAN360

Prototype

What could we glean from hyper local geovisualizations of cancer rates in the United States? This prototype used a variety of data visualization techniques with cancer data for the state of Florida at the zipcode level. A production version was developed by an external studio to launch to the public.

Queso

Web App

What if your class was designed like a game? Queso is a classroom management system for the gameful classroom: assignments map to quests, grades map to levels, the language of games is at the core of the system.
